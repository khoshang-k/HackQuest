FAQ

Do I need to have prior coding experience?

No! Hackathons are for everyone. While coding experience is helpful, there are roles for designers, ideators, and those with business or presentation skills as well.

How many people can be in a team?

Teams can have 3-5 members. If you don’t have a team, don’t worry – you can form one at the event.

What if I don’t have an idea?

No worries! You can join a team that aligns with your skills, and there will be brainstorming sessions to spark creativity.

What are the themes or problem statements?

The hackathon focuses on Blockchain, AI/ML, IoT, and Mixed Reality. Detailed problem statements has been provided under the Problems Tab .

Will there be mentors available?

Yes, we’ll have mentors from various domains to guide you throughout the event.
