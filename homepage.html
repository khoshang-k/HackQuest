Creating Smarter Machines, One Hack at a Time

Welcome to HackQuest, show your amazing ideas and stand a chance to win some amazing goodies. Join us for an unforgettable experience where creativity meets technology! Whether you're a seasoned coder, a designer, or a visionary with big ideas, this is your chance to push the boundaries and bring your ideas to life.

Themes

Mixed Reality

Create a VR training simulation for a specific industry (e.g., healthcare, manufacturing, or aviation) that allows users to practice skills in a realistic virtual environment or Build an AR application that enhances learning by bringing textbooks, diagrams, or historical artifacts to life through interactive 3D models and animations.

AI/ML

Create a model to help healthcare institutions to monitor patient records and provide suggestions or build a model to analyze data after natural disasters to provide real time insights. Else build a model to help finincial institutions detect fraudulent transanctions.

Blockchain

Create a blockchain solution that enables real-time tracking and verification of goods throughout the supply chain. or design a blockchain-based voting system that ensures transparency, security, and verifiability while maintaining voter anonymity. Else create a blockchain-based platform for trading carbon credits that ensures the authenticity and traceability of credits.

IoT

Develop an IoT based device to monitor usage of fertilizers and other agriculture resources. Else create an application for homeowners to track their carbon footprint.

What is HackQuest and Why join Us?

HackQuest is 24 hour long running hackathon will be held on August 24th & 25th , We call for hackers, artists, coders, designers, tech evangelists, creatives, and developers from all disciplines to take a part to win awesome prizes and collaborate with other developers . Throughout the weekend hackers at HackQuest will have unique opportunities to learn from each other trying hands in new technologies. We are determined to have an all-inclusive and diverse group of students attending.
